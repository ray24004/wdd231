About the Project

What is Kart Turismo Colombo?

Kart Turismo Colombo is a digital kart racing game that recreates the real-world map of Colombo, Brazil. The game promotes local tourism and businesses while delivering an engaging experience to players.

Our Mission

We strive to boost Colombo's tourism and economy by creating an interactive game that highlights the city's attractions and businesses. Through in-game incentives, players are encouraged to visit real locations and engage with local establishments.

The Vision Behind the Project

Colombo is often perceived as a "dormitory city" due to its residents commuting to Curitiba. Many of its historical and cultural sites remain underappreciated. This project seeks to change that by raising awareness and fostering engagement through an immersive digital experience.

How the Game Works

Players can explore Colombo's streets in a digital kart, discovering landmarks and racing on tracks inspired by real tourist locations. The game integrates local businesses, allowing players to earn rewards for visiting establishments, with additional benefits for purchases.

Business Model & Revenue Generation

The game follows a freemium model with optional in-game purchases. Additional revenue streams include partnerships with local businesses, virtual advertising, and competitive events linked to city festivals like Festa da Uva.

Development & Future Expansion

Currently in early development, the project aims to grow by enhancing visuals, expanding marketing efforts, and hosting community events to engage both players and businesses.
