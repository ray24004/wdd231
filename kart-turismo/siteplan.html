Kart Turismo Siteplan

Site Name

Kart Turismo - This name represents a kart racing game under development by myself as a game developer, the game project is incubated in the GrapeTech innovation ecosystem of Colombo, Brazil, and intends to promote tourism in the city by featuring virtual kart races over the real map of the city.

Optional domain: kart-turismo.com.br

Site Purpose

The site will be the official source of presentation, development news and feedback for the Kart Turismo project, connecting the dev team with the local community and potential investors. The visitor may find a brief explanation of the game origin and features, updated articles sharing news and status of development, and also fill and submit a contact form

Scenarios

  • What is the origin, intention and results of the game?
  • Which touristic sites of Colombo, Brazil, are represented in the game?
  • How much of the game is ready?
  • How can I playtest the game and offer feedback?
  • Who is developing, suporting or sponsoring the project?
